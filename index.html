Carlos Quijano-Chavez

I’m master student's in Computer Science at the Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul (UFRGS) in Porto Alegre - Brazil, under the supervision of Prof. Carla M. D. S. Freitas and Luciana Nedel. My research focuses especially on the emerging research topic -Immersive Analytics which focuses on applying engaging, embodied display and interaction techniques for data visualization and visual analytics in virtual reality.
